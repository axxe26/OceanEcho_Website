Seals

Seals are marine mammals found in oceans and some freshwater regions worldwide, and they face threats such as climate change, pollution, bycatch in fishing nets, habitat loss, and hunting.

Whales

Whales are large marine mammals found in oceans worldwide, and they face threats including commercial whaling, ship strikes, entanglement in fishing gear, pollution, and climate change affecting their food sources.
